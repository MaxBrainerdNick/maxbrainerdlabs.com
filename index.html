How it works

Simple from day one.

No lengthy discovery phases. No jargon. Just a clear process that gets you from problem to finished training content efficiently.

1

Discovery call (30 min)

We talk through your training gap — who needs to learn what, and what’s gone wrong when they don’t. No prep required on your end.

2

Scope & proposal

You get a plain-language proposal outlining exactly what I’ll build, what format it’ll be in, and what it costs. No surprises.

3

Build & review

I build your content and share it for review. You give feedback, I revise. Most projects wrap in 1–3 weeks depending on scope.

4

Delivery & handoff

You get final files in whatever format works for your team — hosted link, downloadable file, or embedded in your existing tools.
